Pricing

How does codifi Pricing work?

Codifi offers pay-as-per-your-usage pricing without restricting users on features. So there are no confusing pricing tiers - every user will get every feature on Codifi. Organizations will be billed monthly for the resources utilized across all the Networks. Codifi offers best-in-the-market rates for the resources - nodes, and storage. In addition, Codifi offers a free trial for holders to explore Codifi without any cost.
